How to Use Our URL Shortener

1

Paste Your Long URL

Copy any long URL from your browser or application and paste it into our URL shortening input field. Our system accepts URLs of any length and automatically validates them to ensure they are properly formatted and accessible. Make sure to include the full protocol (http:// or https://) for guaranteed proper redirection.

2

Customize Your Short Link (Optional)

Create memorable, branded short URLs using our custom alias feature. Instead of a randomly generated string, specify your own custom alias that reflects your brand or campaign. Custom aliases must be 4-30 characters using letters, numbers, and hyphens. Our system checks availability in real-time.

3

Generate and Share

Click "Shorten URL" to instantly create your shortened link. Our high-performance infrastructure processes requests in milliseconds. Copy your shortened URL with one click and share it across social media, emails, texts, or printed materials. All shortened URLs remain active indefinitely with no expiration date.

4

Track Performance

Monitor your shortened URLs through our private analytics dashboard. Each shortened link comes with a unique, private analytics URL that provides detailed insights into click patterns, geographic distribution, and referral sources. Access real-time analytics to track campaign performance and understand audience behavior.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do shortened URLs remain active?

All shortened URLs created through our service remain active indefinitely. We do not automatically delete or deactivate links due to inactivity. Your shortened links will continue to work for as long as our service is operational, ensuring that your shared links remain accessible to your audience.

What analytics data do you track?

We track total click counts, geographic location data (country and city level), referral sources, timestamps of clicks, and device information. All analytics data is presented through an intuitive dashboard that updates in real-time. Your analytics are completely private and accessible only through your unique analytics URL.

Is my analytics data private?

Yes, your analytics data is completely private. Each shortened URL generates a unique, secret analytics URL that only you can access. We never share your analytics data with third parties, and all personal information is anonymized to protect user privacy while still providing valuable insights into your link performance.

Do you provide QR codes for shortened URLs?

Yes, every shortened URL automatically includes a downloadable QR code. The QR code is generated instantly and can be downloaded in high-resolution PNG format. This feature is perfect for print materials, business cards, event promotions, and any situation where you want to bridge digital and physical marketing.

Are there any restrictions on the URLs I can shorten?

We do not allow shortening of URLs that point to malicious, illegal, or inappropriate content. All URLs are automatically scanned for security threats before being shortened. We reserve the right to block or remove any shortened URLs that violate our terms of service or pose security risks to our users.

Can I edit a shortened URL after creating it?

Currently, shortened URLs cannot be edited once created. The destination URL and custom alias are permanent. If you need to change the destination or alias, you'll need to create a new shortened URL. This ensures consistency and prevents broken links for users who may have already saved or shared your shortened URL.

Is this URL shortener completely free to use?

Yes, our URL shortening service is completely free with no limitations. You can create unlimited shortened URLs, access comprehensive analytics, and generate QR codes without any cost. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core URL shortening functionality will always remain free to use.

How fast is the URL redirection?

Our URL redirection is extremely fast, with average response times under 100 milliseconds. We use optimized server infrastructure and global content delivery networks to ensure that your shortened URLs redirect instantly, providing a seamless experience for anyone clicking your links regardless of their location.
